Welcome to **Trust for Dummies** — a complete introduction to trust in the world of software.

In this series, we take you through:

1. **The Weakest Link** — Why trust is about chains and risks
2. **Certificates & SBOM** — The digital passports and ingredient lists
3. **Zero Trust in Practice** — Implementation for real people
4. **Your Own Trust Strategy** — From theory to action

Each episode builds on the previous one, so you gradually gain a solid understanding of modern security thinking.
