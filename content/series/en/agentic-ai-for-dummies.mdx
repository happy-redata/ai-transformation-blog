Welcome to **Agentic AI for Dummies** — your journey from AI novice to agentic collaborator.

In this series, we take you on a progressive journey through AI-assisted development:

1. **From ChatGPT to GitHub Copilot** — Understand the difference between Ask Mode and Agent Mode
2. **Prompt Engineering** — Learn to let AI write your prompts for you
3. **GitHub for Beginners** — Repositories, Issues, Pull Requests, and Actions
4. **Agentic AI** — When AI works autonomously on your tasks

**Core metaphor:** *The AI Evolution Ladder* — from conversation partner, to helpful assistant, to skilled craftsman working alongside you.

Each episode builds on the previous, giving you a solid understanding of modern AI-assisted development.
