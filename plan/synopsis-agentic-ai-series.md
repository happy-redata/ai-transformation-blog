## Series Overview

> **From Chat to Craft: Your Journey from AI Novice to Agentic Collaborator**

This series takes readers on a progressive journey from basic AI chat interfaces to full agentic development workflows. Each post builds on the previous, moving from familiar consumer experiences to sophisticated developer workflows — all while maintaining the "For Dummies" accessible tone.

**Core Metaphor:** *The AI Evolution Ladder* — from a simple conversation partner (chat), to a helpful assistant (copilot), to a skilled craftsman who works alongside you (agent).
